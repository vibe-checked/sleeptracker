🌙 SleepTracker

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

SleepTracker does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All your sleep data stays on your device.

🔒 Data Collection

SleepTracker is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect any personal information from our users. Specifically:

  • No personal identification data is collected (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No health or biometric data leaves your device
  • No usage analytics or tracking code is included in the app
  • No third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking pixels are embedded
  • No cookies or similar tracking technologies are used

📱 On-Device Data

All sleep data, health metrics, preferences, and settings are stored exclusively on your device. This includes:

  • Sleep duration, quality scores, and efficiency metrics
  • Heart rate, blood oxygen (SpO2), HRV, respiration rate, and wrist temperature readings
  • Sleep stage analysis (Awake, REM, Light, Deep)
  • Readiness scores, sleep fuel, and stress metrics
  • Smart alarm settings and sleep goals
  • Personal notes and emoji annotations
  • Theme preferences and app configuration

This data is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

❤️ Apple Health Integration

SleepTracker may read data from and write data to Apple Health (HealthKit) with your explicit permission. This integration is entirely optional and operates under Apple's strict HealthKit privacy guidelines:

  • Health data accessed through HealthKit is processed only on your device
  • We do not store HealthKit data outside of the app's local storage
  • HealthKit data is never shared with third parties
  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time through Settings > Health > Data Access

📤 Data Export

SleepTracker provides a data export feature that allows you to download your sleep data as a CSV file. This export:

  • Is initiated solely by you and processed entirely on your device
  • Does not transmit data to any server during the export process
  • Produces a file that you control and can share at your discretion

🛡️ Third-Party Services

SleepTracker does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect user data. There are:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics platforms
  • No crash reporting services that transmit data externally
  • No social media integrations
  • No third-party authentication providers

👶 Children's Privacy

SleepTracker does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the app does not collect any personal data from any user, it is inherently compliant with children's privacy regulations including COPPA.

🌍 International Users

Since SleepTracker does not collect, process, or transfer any personal data, the app is compliant with international privacy regulations including GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), PIPEDA (Canada), and other applicable data protection laws. There is no personal data to process, transfer, or delete.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.

If we ever change our data practices to collect personal information, we will notify users through the app and request explicit consent before any data collection begins.
